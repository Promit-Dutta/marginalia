My Very First Note

I've had this wish for a long time to have my own blog, partly because I find it very helpful to keep track of my undersanding of a piece of mathematics.

One of the biggest challenges while learning new math, I feel, is to avoid superficial understanding. Often the feeling that 'I know this' doesn't reflect the reality of understanding. One of the best ways to overcome this is to explain the math to others.

At the moment, I don't have many peers to regularly discuss math with. So, I decided an approach which has the immense ability to reach a greater number of math enthusiasts (who, I hope, are in abundance in the great sea of the internet!) and take their perspective on the math. It is a highly rewarding experience to learn different tools and approaches.

I will try to keep this Blog active throughout the undergraduate years. This page (as you've probably already noticed) will primarily consist of the half-formed ideas, or informal notes, occasional updates. In contrast, the posts will be (atleast tried to be) more rigorous and structured pieces of math, not denying the fact that it can contain errors, which is why the posts have a comment section(Giscus) for the readers, who can point out errors, or provide extended view of the topic, or offer different perspective. In the initial days posts may take longer to come. I may change the UI, introduce new sections (if relevant) as the blog evolves. For now, this is where it begins.

Thank you.
