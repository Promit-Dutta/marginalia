My Very First Note

I've had this wish for a long time to have my own blog, partly because I find it very helpful to keep track of my understanding of a piece of mathematics.

One of the biggest challenges while learning new math, I feel, is to avoid superficial understanding. Often the feeling that "I know this" doesn't reflect the reality of understanding. One of the best ways to overcome this is to explain the math to others.

At the moment, I don't have many peers to regularly discuss math with. So I decided on an approach which has the immense ability to reach a greater number of math enthusiasts (who, I hope, are in abundance in the great sea of the internet!) and take their perspective on the math. It is a highly rewarding experience to learn different tools and approaches.

I will try to keep this blog active throughout the undergraduate years. This page will primarily consist of half-formed ideas, informal notes, and occasional updates. In contrast, the posts will be (at least tried to be) more rigorous and structured pieces of math. In the initial days posts may take longer to come. I may change the UI and introduce new sections as the blog evolves. For now, this is where it begins.

Thank you.
